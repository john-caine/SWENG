Finance - Financial Documentation

The Finance team consisted of Prakruti Sinha as the Finance Manager and Sam Beedell as the Deputy Finance Manager. They were responsible for the Business Plan, time-sheets, budgeting strategies and financial reports at each stage of the project.

The documentation on this page is used to analyse the financial health of eCook and optimise the growth of the business.



Business Plan

The business plan outlines the cost of overheads and labour for the entire project and gives a projection on the loan amount we require. It also includes the most essential components of the project such as module pricing.



Financial Report 1

The first financial report contains a summary of group activities, deliverables achieved, table fo resources and finicial statements to summarise our progress from 13/01/2014 to 14/02/2014



Financial Report 2

The second financial report contains a summary of group activities, deliverables achieved, table fo resources and finicial statements to summarise our progress from 17/02/2014 to 02/03/2014



Financial Report 3

The second financial report contains a summary of group activities, deliverables achieved, table for resources and finical statements to summarise our progress from 03/03/2014 to 09/05/2014



Financial Summary Report

The final financial summary report contains a summary of group activities, deliverables achieved, table for resources and finical statements to summarise our progress from 17/02/2014 to 02/03/2014. It also includes a cash flow report, product pricing methodology cash flow forecast, profit/loss statement and critical analysis.



Timesheets

The timesheets are used to keep track of labour hours per group member, each sheet has been signed off by the Financial Manager to justify the amount of work submitted.



Balance Sheet & Income Statement

The balance sheet provides detailed information about Spoons asses, liabilities and shareholders equity. The income statement provided in the same excel spreadsheet shows how much revenue Spoon will earn over a specified time period, it also shows the cost and expenses associated with earning that revenue.
